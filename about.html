About Us

Our web application aims to support sustainable agriculture and reduce food waste by providing accurate predictions for farmers and vendors. We leverage machine learning techniques to predict the crop suitable for growing by a farmer and the market price prediction. In addition, we provide a platform for vendors to post advertisements for soon-to-expire produce, which can help reduce food waste and provide an opportunity for restaurant owners to purchase fresh produce at a lower cost.

We strive to design a user-friendly interface to help farmers and vendors make informed decisions, while also providing economic benefits for all stakeholders in the food supply chain.
